Explore Modern Stone

We are here to save you time and money by providing you quality stone benchtops at cheapest prices.

1. Buying a complete kitchen/laundry/vanity during renovation usually is a complex process, it always involves cabinet maker/retail store/measurements/consultants/appointments/stonemason/slabs/installation/tiles etc etc etc……………

2. When you visit us, you are visiting a one-stop shop, no hustle and bustle.

3. We source surplus/clearance/discontinued stock from all major stone manufacturers, savings are therefore passed onto you, but rest assured, all of our stone benchtops are of the highest quality quartz stone that are available on this planet.

4. We don’t give you a slab. We actually give you a finished product in store that will probably last a lifetime. Heaps of colors and sizes to choose from. Just bring in your plan and get the job done.

1. Calacatta Stonemason has delivered excellent stonework using various materials including engineered stone, granite and marble for over years...

2. Our scope of work ranging from single residential homes to multiple-property projects. Some of our services include, but are not limited to the supply, manufacturing and installation of:

● Kitchen Bench Tops Vic

● Vanity Top(s)

● Laundry Top

● Fire Place & Surroundings

● Shop Fronts

● Shop Counters

● Stone Splash Backs

3. Calacatta Stonemason endeavours to deliver excellent workmanship. Melbourne home and business owners can rely on us for custom made kitchen tops, table tops, reception desks, vanity tops, fireplaces and more. When it comes to both engineered and natural stone benchtops for various purposes, we offer exceptional range and expertise with product quality guaranteed by our stone supplier partners. Our services cover both domestic and commercial clients in all the above areas including architects, builders and cabinet makers. If you have any doubts about whether we can supply a natural stone or customised benchtop for your Melbourne business, call us to discuss your unique requirements.

4. With extensive experience in the industry, you can expect expertise in the manufacture and installation of your products. Our team is committed to satisfying our clients with superior standards of workmanship, thus providing the highest quality work yet keeping the price of our stone benchtops affordable.

5. Our company believes that kitchen stone benchtops should provide functionality as well as enhance the aesthetic value of your property. Catalogues and expertise consultations are available to tailor to your specific needs and preferences. Whether you want us to suggest a stonework design for your home or commercial property, we have something for you in our collection.
